Our Story

LIHLE PROPERTY AND SHUTTLE SERVICES (PTY) LTD was founded with a vision to provide innovative and flexible solutions in accommodation, transportation, and supply services in the Northern Cape region.

Our research showed that while companies have different departments, few offer in-house stationery and clothing supplies. We seized this opportunity to create a one-stop solution for businesses and individuals in the area.

From our humble beginnings, we've grown into a trusted provider known for our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. Our name "Lihle" means "beautiful" in isiXhosa, reflecting our aspiration to create beautiful experiences for our clients.
